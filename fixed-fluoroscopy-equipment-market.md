<p><p>The fixed fluoroscopy equipment market is categorized into remote-controlled fluoroscopy systems and conventional fluoroscopy systems. Remote-controlled systems enhance precision and reduce operator exposure to radiation by allowing technologists to manipulate the imaging equipment from a distance. In contrast, conventional fluoroscopy systems are traditional setups that require direct operator involvement, often leading to higher radiation exposure. Both types are crucial for various medical applications, including diagnostics and interventional procedures, with remote-controlled systems gaining popularity for their advanced safety and efficiency features.</p></p>
