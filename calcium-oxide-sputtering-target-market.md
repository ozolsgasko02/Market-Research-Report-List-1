<p><p>The Calcium Oxide sputtering target market is categorized into two main types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary type targets are designed for continuous use and enable uniform material deposition over large substrates, enhancing productivity and efficiency. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are fixed in position and generally suited for smaller-scale applications where high precision is critical. Both types cater to the specific needs of various industries, including electronics and optics, impacting the overall performance and quality of thin films produced.</p></p>
