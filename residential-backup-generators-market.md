<p><ul><li>Portable Generators</li><li>Standby Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential backup generators market comprises two main types: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are versatile, easily moved, and typically powered by gasoline, making them suitable for temporary power needs during outages or outdoor activities. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed, automatically activating when the grid power fails, and are usually fueled by natural gas or propane. Standby generators provide seamless, continuous power, ensuring home essential systems remain operational, while portable options offer flexibility for various situations.</p></p>
