<p><p>The cermet potentiometer market is segmented into two primary types: single-turn and multi-turn cermet potentiometers. Single-turn potentiometers allow for a complete rotation of the wiper within a single turn, making them suitable for applications requiring minimal adjustments. In contrast, multi-turn potentiometers permit multiple rotations, providing finer control and a wider range of resistance settings, ideal for precise applications. Both types utilize cermet materials for stability and durability, catering to diverse electronic and industrial uses.</p></p>
