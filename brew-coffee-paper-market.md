<p><p>Brew coffee paper is categorized into non-heat-sealable and heat-sealable markets based on the sealing mechanisms used in packaging. Non-heat-sealable brew coffee paper relies on adhesives or mechanical methods to secure the coffee grounds inside, often offering ease of use and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, heat-sealable brew coffee paper utilizes heat to create airtight seals, enhancing freshness and aroma retention. This latter type is often preferred by premium brands looking to deliver an elevated coffee experience and longer shelf life.</p></p>
