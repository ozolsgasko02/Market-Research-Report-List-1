<p><ul><li>MMA</li><li>MIG/MAG</li><li>TIG</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arc welding inverter market encompasses several types, including MMA (Manual Metal Arc), MIG/MAG (Metal Inert Gas/Metal Active Gas), and TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) welding. MMA is characterized by its simplicity and versatility for various materials. MIG/MAG offers high-speed welding with a continuous wire feed, making it ideal for thicker materials. TIG provides high precision and control, suitable for intricate projects. Other types may include technologies like submerged arc welding and plasma arc welding, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
