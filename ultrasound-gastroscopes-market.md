<p><p>The Ultrasound Gastroscopes market comprises two primary types: Optic Ultrasound Gastroscopes and Linear Array Ultrasound Gastroscopes. Optic Ultrasound Gastroscopes utilize advanced optical systems to provide high-resolution imaging of the gastrointestinal tract, facilitating accurate diagnoses. In contrast, Linear Array Ultrasound Gastroscopes employ a linear array of transducers to capture images, enhancing the assessment of tissue structure and vascularity. Both types are essential for minimally invasive procedures, improving patient outcomes through better visualization and diagnostic capabilities in gastroenterology.</p></p>
