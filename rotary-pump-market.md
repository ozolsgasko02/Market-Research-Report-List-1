<p><ul><li>Gear Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li><li>Moving Vane Pump</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary pump market encompasses various types including gear pumps, screw pumps, moving vane pumps, and others. Gear pumps utilize meshing gears to create flow, offering high efficiency for viscous fluids. Screw pumps employ two or more helical screws to move fluids smoothly and are ideal for consistent flow. Moving vane pumps feature sliding vanes that adjust to pressure changes, providing versatility in handling different fluids. Other pump types may include lobe pumps and peristaltic pumps, each designed for specific applications and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
