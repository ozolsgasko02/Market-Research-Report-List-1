<p><p>Pawn shop software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a pawn shop's computers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but requires higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted online, offering flexibility and scalability, with lower initial investment and automatic updates. This model allows for easier access from multiple devices, enhancing collaboration and streamlining operations for modern pawn shops.</p></p>
