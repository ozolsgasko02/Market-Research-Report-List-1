<p><p>PEEK cranial and craniofacial implants are medical devices made from polyether ether ketone (PEEK), a biocompatible, lightweight, and high-strength polymer. These implants are used in neurosurgery and reconstructive surgery to replace or support cranial and facial structures compromised due to trauma, tumors, or congenital conditions. The advantages of PEEK include its excellent mechanical properties, radiolucency, and compatibility with imaging techniques, making it an ideal choice for diverse surgical applications.</p><p>The PEEK cranial and craniofacial implants market is poised for significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing incidence of cranial and facial injuries, rising awareness of advanced surgical techniques, and technological advancements in implant materials and design. Additionally, the aging population and the rising prevalence of conditions such as tumors are expected to further boost market demand. Innovations in 3D printing technology are also enabling the production of custom implants, enhancing surgical outcomes. As healthcare systems increasingly prioritize patient-centered approaches, PEEK-based solutions are likely to gain further traction in the medical field.</p></p>
