<p><p>The digital ear thermometers market is primarily divided into two types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure body temperature from a distance, allowing for quick and hygienic readings without physical contact. In contrast, contact-type thermometers involve inserting a probe into the ear, providing a direct measurement by detecting the infrared heat emitted from the eardrum. Both types cater to various user needs, emphasizing convenience and accuracy in temperature assessment.</p></p>
