<p><ul><li>Metal Stents</li><li>Polymer Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biliary stent market comprises two primary types: metal stents and polymer stents. Metal stents, often made of stainless steel or nitinol, are durable and suitable for long-term use, offering excellent flow rates and resistance to occlusion. In contrast, polymer stents, typically constructed from biocompatible materials, are more flexible and easier to deploy but may have a shorter lifespan. Both types are utilized in treating bile duct obstructions, each having distinct advantages depending on patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
