<p><ul><li>Manual Film Processor</li><li>Automatic Film Processor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiographic film processors market is primarily divided into manual and automatic film processors. Manual film processors require skilled operators to develop films using a series of chemical baths, offering greater control but requiring more time and effort. In contrast, automatic film processors streamline the process, using automated systems to quickly develop films with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and consistency. Both types serve essential roles in medical and industrial radiography, catering to varying operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
