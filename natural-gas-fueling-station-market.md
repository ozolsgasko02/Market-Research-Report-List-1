<p><p>The natural gas fueling station market comprises two primary types: CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) fueling stations and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) fueling stations. CNG stations compress natural gas for use in vehicles with dedicated engines, catering primarily to light-duty and some medium-duty vehicles. In contrast, LNG stations store natural gas in liquid form, allowing for longer transport distances and serving heavy-duty vehicles and industrial applications. Both play critical roles in the transition to cleaner transportation and energy solutions.</p></p>
