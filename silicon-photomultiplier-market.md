<p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) market is primarily segmented into two types: Monocoque Type and Array Type. Monocoque Type SiPMs feature a single, compact structure that enhances durability and performance in detecting low light levels, making them suitable for applications in medical imaging and scintillation detection. Array Type SiPMs, on the other hand, consist of multiple interconnected detector elements, offering greater scalability and resolution, ideal for high-resolution imaging and large-area detection applications in scientific research and security.</p></p>
