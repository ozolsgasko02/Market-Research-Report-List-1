<p><ul><li>Flanged Swing Check Valves</li><li>Flanged Ball Check Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flanged check valves are critical components in fluid systems, preventing backflow while allowing unidirectional fluid movement. Flanged swing check valves operate using a hinged disc that swings open with forward flow and closes under reverse flow, making them ideal for applications with high flow rates. Flanged ball check valves utilize a spherical ball that seats against a valve port to prevent backflow, offering a quick response and low pressure drop. Both types are essential for maintaining system efficiency and safety in various industries.</p></p>
