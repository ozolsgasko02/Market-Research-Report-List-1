<p><p>The Heated Windshield Glass market primarily comprises two types: Heated Wire Windshield and Heated Coated Windshield. Heated Wire Windshields utilize embedded wire elements to generate heat, effectively melting ice and snow for improved visibility. In contrast, Heated Coated Windshields feature a transparent conductive coating that distributes heat evenly across the surface, offering quicker defrosting without the visual impact of wires. Both technologies enhance driver safety and comfort, catering to the growing demand in regions with harsh winter conditions.</p></p>
