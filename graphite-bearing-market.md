<p><p>The graphite bearing market is primarily categorized into two types: radial bearings and axial bearings. Radial bearings are designed to support loads perpendicular to the shaft, allowing for smooth rotation and reducing friction. They are commonly used in various machinery applications. In contrast, axial bearings handle loads parallel to the shaft, facilitating the application of thrust. Both types benefit from the self-lubricating properties of graphite, enhancing performance and lifespan in demanding conditions across industries such as automotive and manufacturing.</p></p>
