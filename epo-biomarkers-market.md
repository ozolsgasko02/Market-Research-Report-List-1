<p><p>The EPO biomarkers market comprises various types of erythropoietins, each with distinct applications. Recombinant human erythropoietin is crucial for boosting red blood cell production, while Darbepoietin alfa, an extended form, offers prolonged action for patients requiring ongoing therapy. Together, these products cater to diverse clinical needs in anemia management.</p></p>
