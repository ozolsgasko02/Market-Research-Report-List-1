<p><strong>The Static Var Compensator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCR-based SVC</li><li>MCR-based SVC</li><li>TSC-based SVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Var Compensators (SVC) enhance power system stability by regulating voltage and improving reactive power compensation. TCR-based SVC utilizes Thyristor-Controlled Reactors to adjust reactive power dynamically. MCR-based SVC features a combination of a Thyristor-Controlled Reactor and a Thyristor-Switched Capacitor for broader reactive support. TSC-based SVC employs multiple Thyristor-Switched Capacitors for fast response and voltage regulation. Each type caters to different market needs based on system requirements, dynamic response capabilities, and operational flexibility in electrical networks.</p></p>
