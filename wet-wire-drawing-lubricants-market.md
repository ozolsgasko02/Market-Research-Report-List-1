<p><ul><li>Wire Drawing Oil</li><li>Wire Drawing Grease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wet wire drawing lubricants market comprises primarily two types: wire drawing oils and wire drawing greases. Wire drawing oils are liquid lubricants that facilitate smooth metal drawing processes, reducing friction and heat, while enhancing surface finish. They are typically water-soluble or mineral-based. In contrast, wire drawing greases are thicker, semi-solid substances that provide superior adhesion and protection during the drawing process, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Both types are essential in the manufacturing of wires, cables, and other metal products.</p></p>
