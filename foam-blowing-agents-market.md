<p><p>The foam blowing agents market comprises various types, including Hydrocarbons (HC), which are cost-effective and low in environmental impact but flammable. Hydrofluorocarbons (HFC) are non-flammable with good thermal properties but have high global warming potential. Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFC) are transitional agents being phased out due to ozone depletion concerns. Hydrofluoroolefins (HFO) offer low GWP and are used as environmentally friendly alternatives but may be less established in the market. Each type serves specific applications in insulation and refrigeration sectors.</p></p>
