<p><p>The Sterilization Container System market is segmented into Valve Type and Filter Type systems. In contrast, Filter Type systems incorporate filters that allow steam penetration while trapping contaminants, enhancing sterilization effectiveness. Both types cater to the medical and surgical industries, providing reliable solutions for maintaining instrument sterility, reducing the risk of post-operative infections, and improving patient safety.</p></p>
