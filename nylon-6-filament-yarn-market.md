<p><p>The Nylon 6 filament yarn market consists of several types: Flat, Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY), and High-Speed Oriented (HSO) yarns. Flat yarns are typically used for applications requiring low stretch and flat surfaces. POY is semi-oriented, allowing for subsequent processing, while FDY is fully drawn, offering superior strength and uniformity. HSO yarns are designed for high-speed production processes, making them ideal for textiles requiring fast manufacturing without compromising quality. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
