<p><p>The digital 3-channel ECG machine market is categorized into wireless and wired ECG machines. Wireless ECG machines utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data, enabling greater mobility and patient comfort during monitoring. They are ideal for remote patient management and telemedicine applications. In contrast, wired ECG machines connect directly to a computer or monitoring system, providing a stable and reliable data transmission. Typically used in clinical settings, they offer accurate readings but limit patient movement during the procedure.</p></p>
