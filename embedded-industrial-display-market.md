<p><p>The embedded industrial display market is divided into two primary types: touch displays and non-touch displays. Touch displays allow users to interact directly with the screen through touch gestures, enhancing usability in applications like control panels and interactive kiosks. In contrast, non-touch displays rely on physical buttons or remote controls for operation, making them suitable for environments where touch interaction is impractical or for applications requiring minimal user input. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, optimizing functionality and user experience.</p></p>
