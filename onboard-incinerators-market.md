<p><p>The onboard incinerator market comprises two primary types: horizontal and vertical incinerators. Horizontal onboard incinerators are designed to operate with a wider design, allowing for efficient combustion and easier maintenance, making them suitable for larger vessels. In contrast, vertical onboard incinerators utilize a more compact, upright design, optimizing space and enhancing waste processing efficiency, which is beneficial for smaller ships. Both types are essential in managing waste disposal onboard, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
