<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dried Flowers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Drying Flowers</li><li>Press-Drying Flowers</li><li>Embedded-Drying Flowers</li><li>Oven-Drying Flowers</li><li>Glycerine-Drying Flowers</li><li>Freeze-Drying Flowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried flowers market encompasses various types of preservation methods. Air-drying flowers allows natural moisture evaporation, resulting in a rustic look. Press-drying flowers involves flattening them to retain their color and shape, ideal for crafts. Embedded-drying preserves flowers in materials like resin for decorative purposes. Oven-drying uses controlled heat to quickly remove moisture. Glycerine-drying infuses flowers with glycerin, maintaining flexibility and color, while freeze-drying removes moisture by sublimation, preserving the flower’s structure and vibrancy for long-term use.</p></p>
