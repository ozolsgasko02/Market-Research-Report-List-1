<p><p>The snap gage market is primarily divided into inner diameter calipers and outer diameter calipers. Inner diameter calipers are designed for measuring the diameters of cylindrical objects from the inside, allowing for precise evaluations of tubes and holes. In contrast, outer diameter calipers measure the external dimensions of cylindrical parts, ensuring accurate sizing for fitting and assembly. Both types play crucial roles in quality control across various industries, including manufacturing and engineering, facilitating accurate measurements to maintain product standards.</p></p>
