<p><p>The Palm Vein Module market is divided into two primary types: Contact Identification and Non-Contact Identification. Contact Identification involves direct physical interaction, where an individual's palm is placed against a sensor to capture vein patterns. This method often requires close proximity and can ensure high accuracy in identification. In contrast, Non-Contact Identification utilizes advanced imaging technology to capture palm vein patterns without direct contact, offering convenience and hygiene benefits. Both types cater to various security applications, enhancing biometric authentication processes.</p></p>
