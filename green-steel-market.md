<p><strong>The Green Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Produced by Electric Arc Furnace (EAF)</li><li>Produced by Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green steel market consists of various production methods aimed at reducing carbon emissions. Steel produced by Electric Arc Furnaces (EAF) primarily recycles scrap steel using electricity, often sourced from renewable energy, minimizing emissions compared to traditional methods. In contrast, Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE) directly reduces iron ore into steel using electricity to split oxides, generating water as a byproduct, thus further lowering carbon footprint. Both methods represent innovative approaches to sustainable steel manufacturing in the evolving green economy.</p></p>
