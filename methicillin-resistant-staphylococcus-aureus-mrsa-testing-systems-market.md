<p><strong>The Methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus Aureus (MRSA) Testing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostics</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRSA testing systems market is broadly categorized into immunodiagnostics and molecular diagnostics. Immunodiagnostics involve the use of antibodies to detect MRSA antigens, providing results through techniques like enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA). Molecular diagnostics, on the other hand, utilize nucleic acid amplification techniques such as PCR to identify MRSA's genetic material, allowing for rapid and precise detection. Both approaches play crucial roles in clinical settings to ensure effective infection control and management of MRSA outbreaks.</p></p>
