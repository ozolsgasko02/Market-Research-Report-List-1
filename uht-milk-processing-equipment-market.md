<p><strong>The UHT Milk Processing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct UHT</li><li>Indirect UHT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UHT milk processing equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: direct UHT and indirect UHT. Direct UHT processing involves injecting steam into milk, rapidly raising its temperature, and then cooling it instantly, ensuring minimal nutrient loss. Conversely, indirect UHT employs heat exchangers to transfer heat through surfaces, allowing for controlled heating and cooling but may retain more nutrients. Both methods aim to extend shelf life without refrigeration, catering to diverse dairy processing needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
